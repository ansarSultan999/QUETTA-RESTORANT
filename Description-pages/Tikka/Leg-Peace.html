Leg Piece

The leg piece refers to a specific cut of chicken that includes the leg and thigh portions. It is known for its juicy and flavorful meat and is a popular choice among chicken lovers. Here's a description of the leg piece: The leg piece of a chicken consists of the drumstick and the thigh. The drumstick is the lower part of the leg, while the thigh is the upper part. This cut offers a combination of dark and white meat, making it incredibly succulent and rich in flavor.
